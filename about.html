Our Story

AgriConnect was born from a simple observation: farmers were struggling to get fair prices for their produce while consumers were paying premium prices for the same products.

Founded in 2024 by a team of agricultural experts and technology enthusiasts, we set out to create a platform that would eliminate the middlemen and create a direct bridge between farmers and consumers.

We're building a platform to help farmers increase their income while providing consumers with fresh, quality produce at fair prices.
